## 🖌 Overview  

Cinemagraph Pro Professional Suite is a dedicated cinemagraph production environment that gives photographers and creative directors a precise, professional workflow for turning video footage into living images that blur the boundary between photography and motion.

The frame selection tools let you identify the ideal source frames for the still regions of the cinemagraph, ensuring the frozen parts of the image are as sharp and well-exposed as any standalone photograph.

Color grading and adjustment tools apply to the entire composition, letting you develop the visual style of your cinemagraph with the same control you would apply to a still photo in a dedicated editing application.

The preview engine renders the looping animation in real time as you adjust the mask and timing, giving you immediate feedback on how the final composition will look without waiting for an export.

Cinemagraph Pro Professional Suite is the production tool for creatives who want their still photography to carry the energy and attention-holding power of motion, delivered in a format that works everywhere images are shared.
